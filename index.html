What Is a Laser Frenectomy?

A Laser Frenectomy is a modern dental procedure that releases the small band of tissue, known as the frenulum, located under the tongue or upper lip. When this tissue is too tight, it can restrict movement and lead to difficulties with breastfeeding, eating, or speaking. Unlike traditional surgical methods, laser treatment is minimally invasive, virtually pain-free, and requires no stitches. Our experienced Pediatric Dentist in Houston uses a precise dental laser to remove the restriction in just minutes, ensuring exceptional comfort and faster healing Here are two types of Laser Frenectomy.

Labial Frenectomy : A Labial Frenectomy gently releases the tight tissue connecting the upper lip to the gums. This quick laser procedure closes gaps between teeth and protects gum health with minimal discomfort.

Lingual Frenectomy: A Lingual Frenectomy frees the tissue under the tongue that causes tongue-tie. Using advanced laser technology, it improves feeding, speech, and oral movement almost immediately.
